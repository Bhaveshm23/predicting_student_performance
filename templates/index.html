The primary purpose of any educational institution is to ensure that quality education is provided to its students. This project helps in predicting the overall performance of the student by extracting useful available data (student courses records, event participation, extra and co-curricular activities, placements). Assisting them in a timely manner will reduce the number of students failing a particular course and will also indirectly reduce the number of students who are dropping out of their educational program. This form will test your Academic as well as Social Influences and map them to build a model of your performance and then classify it intelligently to get the most accurate reults.                   Fill the below form with almost accurate data to get better results!
